Globe Telecom Launches Digital Thumbprint Program at CebuTech Danao Campus

CTU - Danao Campus 3 min read • Aug 29, 2024
350 10

Globe Telecom's Digital Thumbprint Program made a significant impact at the CTU Danao Campus on August 29, 2024, as the company spearheads an engaging seminar focused on educating the youth about digital citizenship and the responsible use of technology. Held at the Kadasig Gym, the event drew students-leaders for a day of insightful discussions and interactive activities. The event is spearheaded by the Student Development Office and the Supreme Student Government.

Dr. Sarah Jane Colina, the Student Development Director delivers her inspiring welcome remarks, emphasizing the importance of digital literacy in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape. “We are excited to collaborate with Globe Telecom to provide our students with the tools and knowledge they need to navigate the digital world safely and responsibly,” Dr. Colina said.

The seminar featured Miss Am Orocay, a renowned advocate for digital citizenship, as the primary resource speaker. She delivered an engaging presentation on the essentials of online etiquette, privacy, and security. Ms. Am said, “Your information is valuable, which needs to be protected. As the world increasingly embraces digital technology, staying informed and cautious about how you handle your personal data becomes crucial.” Her session was interactive, including a dynamic question-and-answer segment where students had the opportunity to test their knowledge and engage directly with the expert.

Adding a lively touch to the event, Globe personnel conducted a series of trivia quizzes, offering various prizes to participants. These activities were designed to reinforce the seminar's key messages while ensuring a fun and memorable experience for everyone involved.

Philip Maglangit, the SSG President, delivered the closing remarks, expressing his gratitude to Globe Telecom and all participants. “Today's seminar has been incredibly enlightening. We are grateful to Globe Telecom for their commitment to empowering our youth with vital digital skills,” Maglangit stated.

The event concluded with a group photo opportunity, capturing the collective enthusiasm and success of the day. The Digital Thumbprint Program at CTU Danao Campus not only provides valuable education but also fostered a sense of community and shared responsibility among the students.
