Cebu Technological University Danao Campus hosts 15th Kampo Kalikasan with the theme "Building Peaceful and Sustainable Communities: Nurturing Life Beyond the Pandemic." Spearheaded by the SAVE ME Movement in partnership with Cebu Technological University (CTU), the event gathered school officials, local government units (LGU's), Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM) and student leaders from various higher education institutions across the Philippines which is scheduled from September 20-23, 2024.

Dr. Joseph C. Pepito, CTU University President, delivered an inspiring keynote address, emphasizing the crucial role of education in fostering sustainable practices.

Over the next four days, student participants will engage in Structured Learning Experiences aimed at deepening their understanding of sustainability and environmental stewardship. This initiative underscores the importance of preserving nature, aligning with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which advocate for responsible consumption and environmental protection.

Student leaders are vital in this endeavor, as they not only serve as agents of change within their communities but also inspire their peers to embrace sustainable practices. Their active involvement is essential for creating a culture of peace and environmental awareness that extends beyond the pandemic, ultimately nurturing a resilient future for all.
